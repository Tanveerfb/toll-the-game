# DUKE — Character Design Sheet

*(Locked 2026-07-07 from Tanveer's direction. Supersedes the lore-derived look used for the v1/v2 art.)*

## Concept
Main character of the story. Design must carry protagonist presence — confident, distinctive silhouette, reads as "the lead" at a glance.

## Physical Appearance
- **Build:** Bulkier than v2 art — visibly muscular, broad shoulders, athletic martial-artist frame.
- **Hair:** Dark blue. **Mid taper fade + textured quiff**, spiky on top in a DBZ-character way — sharp upward texture, clean faded sides.
- **Eyes:** Teal/blue, sharp and determined.
- **Outfit:** Mix of **martial arts gi + combat-enabled suit** — gi silhouette (open top, belt/sash) fused with tactical armor elements: reinforced under-layer, armored bracers/plating. Keeps his magenta + dark color identity from the v2 card.
- **Element FX:** Swirling blue water around fists/body (unchanged).

## Personality (art direction)
Protagonist energy: confident grin or resolute stare, dynamic hero pose. Not brooding, not generic — give him character.
