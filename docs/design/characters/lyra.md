# LYRA — Character Design Sheet

## Concept
An original character built by blending specific physical traits and one personality thread from three existing characters: Ganyu (Genshin Impact), Kefla base form (Dragon Ball Super), and Jericho (The Seven Deadly Sins).

## v2 Visual Lock (2026-07-07 — Tanveer's concept art)
Supersedes the trait-blend below for the card look. Source images: `refs/lyra-concept-1.png`, `refs/lyra-concept-2.jpg`.
- **Hair:** Dark blue/indigo, swept into a **high ponytail with a red hair tie**, loose strands framing the face.
- **Eyes:** Light red/crimson-brown, narrow and stern — composed, unimpressed default.
- **Skin:** Pale.
- **Outfit:** Crimson/burgundy frill-necked top with short sleeves; **woven bronze/gold waist sash**; white/grey skirt (pleated) with a **long crimson back-drape/overskirt**; bronze forearm bracers; red fingerless gloves; burgundy boots.
- **Weapon:** Recurve **bow** trailing red ribbons; red shard/petal particles around her.

## Physical Appearance

### Hair
- **Style (from Ganyu):** Long hair swept into an asymmetrical, high side ponytail — gathered mostly to one side and tied up, with a blunt fringe across the forehead framing the face.
- **Color (from Jericho):** A lavender-into-silver-blue blend — cool, pale purple tones that shift toward silvery-blue depending on lighting.

### Eyes & Face
- **Eye structure (from Ganyu):** Narrow, elegant almond-shaped eyes with slightly upturned outer corners — gives a composed, refined baseline look.
- **Face & eye intensity (from Kefla):** Sharper, more angular underlying bone structure (defined cheekbones, slightly narrowed brow) laid under the almond shape, giving the eyes a fierce, competitive edge rather than a purely serene one. Think: calm shape, fierce intent.
- **Expressions (from Jericho):** Default range leans toward a prideful smirk and a confident, chin-up set of the mouth; flashes of fluster or indignation when her pride gets challenged; a sharp, defiant glare when provoked into a fight.

### Build
- **From Kefla:** Athletic, lean-muscular Saiyan-style build — toned arms and core, built for speed and agility over bulk. Reads as combat-capable at a glance, not bulky.

### Outfit
- **Blended from Ganyu + Kefla:** A fitted, qipao-inspired silhouette (high collar, structured bodice, side slit for leg movement) like Ganyu's, but reworked with combat-practical elements from Kefla's battle outfit — sleeveless or cropped-sleeve cut, reinforced fabric/light armor panels at the shoulders and waist, fingerless gloves, and combat boots in place of anything purely decorative. Elegant silhouette, built to actually fight in.

---

## Personality
- **Boastful and confident** — talks herself up, enjoys being seen as impressive, rarely doubts herself out loud.
- **Not battle-hungry** — she doesn't go looking for fights or thrive on violence for its own sake.
- **Battle-capable** — when a fight does come to her, she's genuinely skilled and dangerous, not just talk.
- **Fast learner** — picks up new techniques, patterns, and opponents' habits quickly; adapts mid-fight rather than relying on a fixed style.
