# SARA — Character Design Sheet

## Concept
A human girl whose easygoing, cat-loving exterior hides a sharp tongue and real protectiveness underneath.

---

## Physical Appearance
- **Hair:** Long platinum-white hair (from Alexia). Canon styling uses pigtails tied with a black ribbon — easy to keep or swap for something looser/more casual.
- **Eyes:** Dark brown, near-black.
- **Build:** Slender, average height.
- **Style:** Modest, casual-modern — no hijab. Think a fitted hoodie or long-line top layered over loose trousers or a midi skirt, built for ease of movement. A few subtle cat-fashion touches (a soft choker, paw-print or whisker stitching, a hood with small cat-ear points) without going full costume.

---

## Personality
- **Alexia (primary):** Polished and proper in public — polite, composed, says the right things. Around people she's actually comfortable with, that drops fast: sharp-tongued, teasing, a little smug, occasionally outright sadistic in a "I'm enjoying this too much" way. Some real insecurity sits under the bravado, even if she'd never admit it.
- **Alpha (secondary):** Underneath all the teasing, genuinely loyal and protective of the people she's claimed as hers. Calm and composed when it actually matters — the teasing stops the second something real is at stake.
- **Salos (light seasoning):** Lazy and easily bored when she's off the clock. Won't lift a finger she doesn't have to, and will absolutely let someone else deal with a problem if she can get away with it. Reads as classic cat behavior more than as a flaw.
