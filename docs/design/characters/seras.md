# SERAS — Character Design Sheet
*Main Villain*

## Concept
A fairy-human hybrid who leads from inside human society itself. Her real strength stays hidden in plain sight — most people who meet her never learn she's anything but a sharp, capable authority figure. The reveal of what she actually is happens only when she decides to let it.

---

## Civilian / Authority Form (Default)
This is the form everyone sees. Indistinguishable from an ordinary human — by design, not by accident.

- **Ears:** Naturally pointed (elf-like), but her hybrid physiology is under her direct control, and she keeps them rounded/human-shaped at will.
- **Eyes:** True color is light reddish; normalized down to an unremarkable, ordinary human shade whenever she's in this form.
- **Hair:** Long, asymmetrical white-silver hair — same in both forms. Unusual, but not something that on its own reads as inhuman.
- **Outfit:** Sharp, tailored professional wear befitting someone who leads an authority or agency — clean lines, understated, commands a room through competence rather than spectacle.
- **Demeanor:** Calm, composed, exactly as authoritative as her position calls for — and not a hint more. Reads as completely trustworthy.

**Why she stays here:** Not to dodge trouble — it's a tactical choice. Passing as fully human lets her move through society, gather intel, get close to a target, or strike without anyone seeing it coming until it's too late.

---

## True / Battle Form
What's underneath, revealed only when she chooses to drop the disguise.

- **Ears:** Natural elf-like points, no longer hidden.
- **Eyes:** True light reddish color; can spark faintly with electricity in the moment before she strikes.
- **Hair:** Same white-silver hair, now styled/accessorized so part of it forms small horn-like shapes near the crown — a clear fae marker.
- **Power manifestation:** Branching, crackling lightning surges across her body when she gets serious, lifting her hair and intensifying her whole presence.
- **Outfit:** Dark, structured silhouette blending kimono-armor formality with a fitted, battle-ready cut — high collar, sharp tailored lines, a flared cape or skirt-piece for drama in motion.
- **Demeanor:** Cold, composed, narcissistically certain her version of order is correct — the same authority she carries in civilian form, just unmasked and amplified.

---

## Weapon & Combat Style
- Primary weapon: a lightning-channeling polearm/staff — strikes land as both physical force and electric burst.
- In an all-out moment, she can draw a secondary blade from the staff for one decisive, devastating strike.

---

## Personality
- **Calculating and patient:** Prefers to let situations and people unravel themselves rather than act directly.
- **Cold authority beneath the human mask:** Genuinely convinced her cruelty serves a "greater" order.
- **Strategic, not insecure:** Blending in is a weapon, not a hiding spot. She's never uncomfortable being seen as ordinary — she's using it. Whether she's pulling information out of someone who has no idea what she is, or closing the distance for a kill no one saw coming, the disguise is always doing work.
