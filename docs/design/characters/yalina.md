# YALINA — Character Design Sheet

## Concept
A Pakistani girl whose strength is built on heart-led instinct and quiet endurance — sharp enough to see the truth, strong enough to stay standing once she does.

---

## Physical Appearance
*(based on movie-Yalina, Dhurandhar)*
- **Hair & eyes:** Dark hair, dark brown eyes — realistic, no stylization.
- **Skin tone:** Warm brown.
- **Build:** Average height, youthful build.
- **Style:** Blends traditional elegance with modern, natural simplicity — rich fabrics, fine jewelry, and lehenga/kurta-style formal pieces for her composed, high-status side; softer, simpler everyday wear for her more vulnerable, unguarded moments. No hijab, matching the source look.

---

## Personality
*(based on movie-Yalina, Dhurandhar)*
- **Heart-led:** Trusts instinct and emotion over cold calculation — she feels her way through a situation before she reasons through it.
- **Stubborn and strong-willed:** Pushes back against expectations placed on her, especially by family or authority.
- **Bright and emotionally intelligent:** Warm, perceptive, good at reading people — the kind of presence that gets even guarded people to open up.
- **Fiercely loyal:** Once she commits to someone, she stays — even after learning a painful truth.
- **Resilient:** Doesn't break under pressure so much as quietly adapt and keep going.
- **Sharp and aspirational:** Intelligent, with ambitions of her own beyond whatever role she's been handed.

---

## Playstyle Concept
A strategic buff-stacker: she accumulates stacking buffs whenever her *allies* attack, then cashes the entire stack into one massive strike before the cycle resets. Power is tied to team tempo, not just her own actions — a build-and-release loop rather than a steady damage source.

**Design notes from our discussion:**
- Decide on a stack cap to set a clean power ceiling (uncapped scaling risks spiraling in long fights).
- She'll need some defensive or evasive tool while stacking, or she becomes a pure "kill her first" target.
- Worth deciding whether the big release is guaranteed on stack-completion or can still miss/be blocked — changes how skill-expressive vs. reliable she feels.
